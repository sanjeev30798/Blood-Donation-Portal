Who are eligible to donate blood?

  • Any healthy adult, both male and female, can donate blood.
  • Men can donate safely once in every three months while women   can donate every four months.
  • Donor should be in the age group of 18 to 65 years
  • The donor should not be less than 45kg in weight
  • Temperature and blood pressure of donor should be normal
  • Donor should have delivered child 1 year ago and stopped lactation
  • Haemoglobin should not be less than 12.5 grams
  • Should not have got tatoo in last 12 months
  • Should not have any form of cancer
  • Should not have HIV, AIDS, Hepatatis B,C ,Tuberculosis
  • Should not have heart diseases
